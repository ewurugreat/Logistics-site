Enjoy Hassle Free Shipping With HEYLogistics

STANDARD AIRFREIGHT FROM USA TO NIGERIA:

Air freight from USA to Nigeria takes 3-15 business days.

Our consignment leaves at 12pm on Friday of every week, and then undergoes

TSA check over the weekend to be airlifted on Monday of the following week.

Please note that counting starts from the Monday when it is Airlifted not Friday when it leaves our Warehouse.

Our weekly cut off is on Thursday 12PM.

Any package arriving at our facility after the cut off time meets up with the next freighting cycle i.e the following week.

SHIPPING FEE & MINIMUM WEIGHT:

Shipping fee is $3.99/per pound, shipping fees covers Customs duties, taxes, VAT, and clearing fees.

Packages below 5lb are charged at a fixed flat rate of $30.

Volume Weight applies to charges in a case where the Volume weight is Higher than the Actual Weight.

For an accurate shipping cost, take measurement of the length width and height of the package and divide it by 166.

e.g 23.00 x 21.00 x 21:00 / 166
